    Why UCOOK?

    Cook like a chef

    Whip up restaurant-quality meals using fresh ingredients and simple-to-follow recipes. With 24 new dinners and 4 new lunch dishes every week, you’ll hone your kitchen skills and build your culinary repertoire.

    Saves you money

    With tasty meals from less than R65 per person, we take care of all the portioning and measuring so you only get exactly what you need. No wastage, no queueing, and delivery straight to your door!

    Fresh produce

    We source only the best and freshest ingredients that we can find from local farms and businesses. We choose reliable suppliers and aim for organic and free range wherever it’s possible.

    Conscious decisions

    We're all about making better eating choices, supporting local businesses, and reducing our carbon footprint. We consider what we do and the impact that our choices will have on the planet.

    No commitment

    You can pause at any time for flexibility that meets your needs. When you go away or have events planned for your evenings, you can pause without penalty or even change your delivery address.

    Convenience

    Say goodbye to meal-planning panic; we've taken the hassle out of deciding what’s for lunch and dinner! Our range of options ensures every meal is deliciously simple.
